What is Soundchain?

Soundchain is a music streaming platform that uses blockchain technology to provide a fair trade music listening experience. In order to pay the creators of music, instead of showing you ads, we borrow your device's CPU to mine cryptocurrency. The cryptocurrency you generate is then sent to the artist that you listened to. It's that simple. In this way, you have a uninterruped music experience and the artists get paid directly. Now, if you don't want to lend your cpu that's all right. Soundchain works on a prepaid fashion as well. Load any amount of money into your account and pay the artists as you go, no subscriptions, no chains
